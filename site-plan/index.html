Dinosaur Discovery – Website Plan

Site Name

Dinosaur Discovery

This name was chosen because the site is focused on helping users explore and learn about dinosaurs in a fun and interactive way. It evokes a sense of curiosity and adventure. A possible domain name could be dinosaur-discovery.org.

Site Purpose

The website is an educational and interactive platform where users can explore a curated list of dinosaurs, learn facts about each species, and share their favorite dinosaurs through a form. It is designed to appeal to students, teachers, and general dinosaur enthusiasts of all ages.

Scenarios

  • What are some cool facts about the T-Rex or Velociraptor?
  • How can I submit my favorite dinosaur and see it featured?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color – Forest Green (#2d6a4f): Used for headings, buttons, and navigation background to reflect a natural, prehistoric theme.
  • Accent Color – Amber (#fcbf49): Used for highlights, hover effects, and icons to draw attention to interactive elements.

Typography

  • Heading Font – "Bebas Neue", sans-serif: Used for all main headings and section titles to provide a bold, eye-catching look.
  • Body Font – "Open Sans", sans-serif: Used for paragraph text, buttons, and form inputs for readability and a clean appearance.
